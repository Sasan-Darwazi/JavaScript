The Difference Between an HTMLCollection and a NodeList.
A NodeList and an HTMLColleciton is very much the same thing.

Both are array-like collections (lists) of nodes (elements) extracted from a document. The nodes can be accessed by index numbers. The index starts at 0.

Both have a length property that returns the numerb of element in the list(collection).

an HTMLCollleciton is a collection of document Elements.

A NodeList is a colllection of document nodes (element nodes, attribute nodes, and text nodes).

HTMLCollection items cna be accessed by their name, id, or index number.

NodeList items can only be accessed by their index number.
